About the job
What To Expect
North America Business Planning provides the critical thinking, insights, plans, and cross functional alignment required for Tesla to continue to aggressively scale and operate with optimal efficiency. We stay focused on aligning the highest-level company priorities with strong day-to-day operations and build capabilities to power hyper-growth.

In this role, you will directly support Tesla’s customer facing expansion plans and measure the performance of this portfolio. You will work directly with Service, Sales, Delivery, Collision and Customer Support leaders to create and implement market growth strategies and operations improvements. Your analyses and creative thinking will guide where we go next.

What You'll Do

Combine quantitative and qualitative analyses to explain factors driving customer demand and utilize this information to forecast capacity needs within Tesla’s customer facing portfolio of locations and teams
Track and measure the performance of existing locations and teams and partner with operations leaders to identify improvement opportunities
Analyze field feedback on operations systems and provide insights to the business and IT teams which highlight potential for enhancements which will improve efficiency
Build business cases and analyses with Program Managers to support process improvement initiatives they are driving
Create high-quality analyses and summaries for engagement with executive leadership


What You'll Bring

Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering, Economics, Business, or Data Analytics or equivalent experience
Significant experience in data analytics, scenario planning, predictive modeling
Expert in data processing, including analysis of big data using Excel and SQL; experience using VBA, Python, or R is ideal
Experience building data visualizations using Tableau Desktop or similar
Experience collaborating with a wide variety of stakeholders
Self-starter who can lead, manage, and prioritize multiple projects and workstreams in a fast-paced environment
Must be available to work from the Palo Alto, CA office regularly
